Similarity retrieval

This application is used to perform patient similarity retrieval. Similar patients are shown close to one another on an interactive 2D plot, and statistics relating to selected patients are displayed.

Patient trace

This application is used to visualise a patient's condition over time. The patient's laboratory results are dimensionally reduced and shown on a 2D interactive plot.
